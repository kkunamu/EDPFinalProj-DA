About Us

The Department of Agriculture (DA) is the government agency at the forefront of promoting agricultural development and ensuring food security across the Philippines. It supports farmers, fisherfolk, and agri-based enterprises through inclusive policies, modern technologies, and targeted investments — with a strong focus on sustainability and community empowerment.

Guided by the vision of Masaganang Agrikultura, Maunlad na Ekonomiya, the DA works hand in hand with local government units and private sectors to build a resilient, competitive, and productive agriculture sector. From seed distribution to infrastructure and training, the DA is committed to uplifting rural livelihoods and nourishing every Filipino home.
